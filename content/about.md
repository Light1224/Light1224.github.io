# Hi, I’m Shamit.

I’m an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at BITS Pilani (Goa), with a Data Science minor. I like building things that are fast, measurable, and explainable — mostly in C++, Python, and systems-oriented workflows.

## What I work on

- High-performance systems (kernel-level optimization, memory layout, SIMD, threading)
- Quantitative research (market structure, regime detection, reproducible pipelines)
- Practical ML infrastructure (clean data flow, strict validation, deployable outputs)

## Selected work

- **GEMM Kernel (Apple M2, C++20):** built from naive baseline to blocked + packed + NEON + multithreaded execution, reaching **231 GFLOP/s** (about **95% of OpenBLAS**) and over **200x** speedup vs baseline.
- **Spectral Volatility Research (NIFTY-100):** used RMT + PCA + structural validation to isolate signal from noise, with strict walk-forward testing showing strong risk-state discrimination (**AUC ~0.90** at 10-day horizon).

## Beyond projects
