Overview

This page is the front door to the full NIFTY-100 volatility structure project log.

The write-up is structured as a technical blog series: each page explains one stage of the pipeline, what was measured, what held up under checks, and where the limits appeared. Instead of treating figures as decoration, every section ties conclusions to exported metrics and artifact plots.

The journey runs from transformed volatility panels to strict-causality predictive diagnostics. Along the way, it covers correlation geometry, eigenspectrum concentration, Marchenko-Pastur filtering, factor interpretation, stability testing, regime segmentation, falsification experiments, and reproducibility audits.
